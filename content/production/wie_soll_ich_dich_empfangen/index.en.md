**A centuries-old hymn reimagined.**\
**Music that feels familiar, yet full of surprises.**

The newly crafted text, developed in the spirit of the original, tells of a God who does not remain distant but comes to us: as King, as Friend, and as Comfort in times of sorrow. And it challenges us to carry God’s light into the darkness with our own lives.

The modern gospel arrangement and the heartfelt voice of Krís Madarász invite listeners to hear the hymn anew – and perhaps rediscover the original version with fresh ears.
